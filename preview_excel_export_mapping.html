Q1: yardi_data refresh — write from product OR leave as imported?
The product stores budget_lines which is essentially what's in yardi_data (2) at the time of import. After the FA does YTD updates / accruals / unpaid edits, the product is the source of truth, but the Excel's yardi_data tab still reflects the original import.

Options:
(A) Rewrite yardi_data on export from budget_lines current state — VLookup formulas auto-recompute and the file is self-consistent.
(B) Leave yardi_data as imported, only overwrite specific cells. Faster, less risky, but yardi_data lags.
(C) Add a third "yardi_data_current" tab with the product's current state, leave the original. Auditor sees both.

Q4: Energy sheets — write per-line overrides explicitly, or just assumptions?
Most energy sheets are "rate × prior year usage = forecast" formulas. The FA mostly edits the rate (an assumption). Sometimes FA overrides a specific line's forecast.

Options:
(A) Only write the rate assumption; let the Excel formulas do the rest. Cleanest.
(B) Write both rate AND every line override the FA made.

Rec: B — the FA's overrides are part of the product's truth and need to land somewhere on the worksheet (probably the "Forecast" column with a "FA edit" comment marker).
