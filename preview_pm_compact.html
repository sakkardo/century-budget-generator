What changed (no columns removed)

  • 9 visual treatments → 3. Reference cells become plain dim monospace text on transparent background (no boxes, no borders). Decision cells stay loud (blue Proposed, colored pills). Same information density, less noise.
  • fx stickers removed on PM portal. Estimate and Forecast are read-only here (only FA can edit those). The green-border fx affordance was misleading.
  • "No change" button → inline = icon. Sits right of empty Proposed inputs at the same height. No more row-height jitter when only some rows have the button.
  • Subtotal rows render derived pills + Proposed total. Fixes the "Supplies and Repairs don't line up" complaint — the gap was empty subtotal Inc % / Inc $ cells.
  • Column zones with subtle dividers. Identity | Reference | Decision | Diagnostic | Notes. Five subtle vertical dividers tell the eye where one "zone" ends. Reading flows left-to-right without needing labels.
  • Notes column loses yellow border on high-delta rows. Replaced with a 6px red dot at the left edge. Less competing color, still flags missing context.
  • Increase $ cell loses the bright green background. It was the loudest cell on the page in the live build; under single-entry it's a derived pill anyway, doesn't need the highlight.
  • Click-to-drill behavior on GL/Description unchanged. Same muscle memory. Same invoice table appears below the row when clicked.
