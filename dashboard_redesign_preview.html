🧭 The three principles

1 · AGGREGATE
One Action Center at the top. Every "you need to do X" signal funnels in. Ordered by urgency. Each row has ONE button — Review, View, Continue, Upload. No more than 6 items shown at once; rest collapse behind "show all".
2 · COMPRESS
All passive status (pipeline, period, audit, sources) compresses into ONE thin strip. Glanceable, not anxious. Anything red/amber in the strip auto-promotes itself to an Action Center row.
3 · GET OUT OF THE WAY
The Budget Workbook fills the rest of the screen. KPI cards hide behind a button (rarely consulted). Section headers ("Budget Workbook") deleted — the page IS the workbook. The FA sees data immediately.

Total: ~6-8 hours. Zero new endpoints — everything aggregates from data already on /api/dashboard/<entity> and /api/readiness/<entity>. No data risk. Behavior under the hood unchanged — this is purely a re-layout of what's already there.
