Design Shotgun FA Dashboard — Drawer-Trigger Variants

Building 148 · Real data · Three trigger patterns for the Action Center drawer
The question: Where does the "open Action Center" trigger live so the workbook stays clean but blockers are still impossible to miss?

A · Quiet Pill

Small button in the top-right nav. Red badge with count. Subtle until you read it. Doesn't steal focus from data. Best if FAs work mostly without blockers.

B · Status Bar

Horizontal red-tinted strip above the tabs. "● 2 blockers · 4 warnings — click to review." Hard to miss. Disappears when zero issues. Best if blockers are common.

C · Floating FAB

Circular button bottom-right with badge. Workbook surface is fully data. Trigger floats on top. Best for total minimalism but trigger never goes away.
