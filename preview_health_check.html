How this would live in the app: a "Run Health Check" button shows up on every building's dashboard. One click runs 15+ checks against the building's actual data. Green check = good. Yellow = warning, FA should review. Red = blocker, fix before moving forward. A separate portfolio view runs all 143 buildings at once.
