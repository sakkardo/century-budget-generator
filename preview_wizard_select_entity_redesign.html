What changed and why

  1. Removed: helper banner DEL "Each entity has its own budget timeline" carried no information. The chip row + count is the orientation.
  2. Removed: "All Entities" dropdown DEL Redundant with the chip filter. The "29 of 147" count now sits inline on the active chip.
  3. Removed: Status column DEL Every row in a filtered view says the same thing the active chip says. Three signals for one fact.
  4. Reworked: hero is state-aware REWORK When 0 buildings are ready and N are in audit review, the hero pushes toward "clear the audit queue" — the binding constraint. When N are ready, hero pushes toward "Build all N". Today's hero just says "11 in progress" without telling you what to do with that.
  5. Reworked: Data status tiles → "Missing" text label REWORK Five tiny red boxes per row don't communicate well at a glance. For "Waiting" tiers, a single red text label "B · E · Y · A · Au" is faster to scan. Tiles stay for "In progress" and "Built" tiers where the data does vary across cells.
  6. Reworked: Next-step button is always actionable REWORK No more greyed "Waiting for files" tautologies. When you can't build, the action becomes "Email PM", "Open SP folder", "Upload audit". The FA always has a move.
  7. Added: source legend ADD A compact strip above the table says what B / E / Y / A / Au stand for. Today the column header just lists "(B·E·Y·A·Au)" with no decoder — new FAs see five red boxes and have to guess. Now: "B = 2026 Approved Budget · E = Expense Distribution · Y = YSL (Yardi) · A = AP Aging · Au = 2025 Audit PDF".
  8. Added: Waiting column ADD Days since last activity. Red text when >21 days. Default sort within blocked tiers is oldest-first so the rotten ones surface.
  9. Added: FA filter ADD Click an FA name in a row to filter. Or use the FA dropdown next to the chips. Today there's no way to focus on "just my buildings".
  10. Added: portfolio summary in title ADD "147 buildings · 10 built · 0 ready" replaces the "FINANCIAL ANALYST" label. Cheap orientation, doesn't add a band.
  11. Compressed: filter band is one row REWORK Chips + FA filter + search all on one row. Saves ~80px of vertical space, shows 2 more rows above the fold.
